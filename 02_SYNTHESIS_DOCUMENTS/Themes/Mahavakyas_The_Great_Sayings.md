## 4. The Path of Realization

The Mahavakyas are not meant to be simply believed, but to be realized through a three-fold process:

1.  **Shravana (Hearing)**: Listening to the teachings of the Upanishads from a qualified guru.
2.  **Manana (Reflection)**: Contemplating the meaning of the Mahavakyas, using reason and logic to remove all doubts.
3.  **Nididhyasana (Meditation)**: Deep, sustained meditation on the truth of the Mahavakyas, leading to the direct experience of non-duality.
