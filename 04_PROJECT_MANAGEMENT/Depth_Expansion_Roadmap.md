   - A new table `verses` to store individual verses from key texts (Upanishads, Gita, Sutras).

2. **Commentary Database Table**:
   - A new table `commentaries` to link commentaries to specific verses.
   - Fields: `verse_id`, `commentator_id`, `commentary_text`.

3. **Cross-Reference System**:
   - A new table `cross_references` to link related concepts and verses across different texts.
   - Fields: `source_verse_id`, `target_verse_id`, `relationship_type`.

4. **Enhanced Depth Scoring Algorithm**:
   - The depth score will be updated to reflect the new verse-level and commentary data.
   - Depth will be calculated based on:
     - % of verses with translation
     - % of verses with multiple commentaries

5. **New Validation Scripts**:
   - Scripts to validate the integrity of the verse and commentary data.
